⚠️ Important Notes
  • Do not include your passwords

🎯 Common Scam Types

💰 Financial Scams

Fake investment opportunities, lottery winnings, or requests for money transfers.

🔐 Identity Theft

Requests for personal information, SSN, bank details, or account credentials.

🛠️ Tech Support

Fake tech support calls claiming your computer is infected or needs repair.

💕 Romance Scams

Fake romantic relationships designed to extract money or personal information.

🏛️ Authority Impersonation

Fake calls from IRS, police, courts, or other government agencies.

🎁 Prize Scams

Fake notifications about winning prizes, contests, or sweepstakes.

🚨 Emergency Resources

💰 Financial Loss

Contact your bank immediately and report to the FBI's Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3).

🔐 Identity Theft

Report to IdentityTheft.gov and place fraud alerts on your credit reports.

📧 Email Scams

Forward phishing emails to your email provider and report to the Anti-Phishing Working Group.
